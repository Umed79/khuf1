Khuf Valley is located in Rushan district, in a very picturesque gorge. Its altitude varies from 2,700 to 3,000 meters above the sea level. The Valley has a rich historical and cultural heritage, landscape, with climatic and natural conditions that are very suitable to develop eco-tourism. There are good perspectives to organize and develop extreme tourism, trekking, mountaineering, photo tours and skiing. Development of tourism in Khuf is a very relevant and promising direction for a valley with a rich history and culture.
