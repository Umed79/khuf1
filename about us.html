Umed GUEST HOUSE consists of 2 floors and 5 rooms. Designed for 10-15 people. On the first floor there are 3 rooms, one of which is decorated in the Pamiri style (Pamir house), a veranda, a kitchen and a toilet room. The second floor has 2 rooms, a toilet and a veranda. The bathroom is outside. Our guest house has its own territory with a small forest, quite suitable to set up tents.
UMED GUEST HOUSE offers tourists a room, breakfast and dinner, with the price of $ 20 for daily subsistence per person.
People in Khuf Valley have opportunity to provide travelers with their own products, feed them with traditional mountain food, play live music, sell souvenirs and various hand-made products. It is a place where one would want to return again.
